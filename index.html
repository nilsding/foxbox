About foxbox
foxbox is nilsding's personal player for tracker music files, based on libopenmpt. foxbox supports MOD, XM, IT, S3M, MPTM, and most other module formats you can throw against OpenMPT. foxbox is still under development. foxbox is open source. Star foxbox on GitHub now!
